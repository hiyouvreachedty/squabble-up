---

## Design Principles

1. Dark by default — premium, cinematic energy
2. Typography-first — let words carry visual weight before imagery
3. One accent color — red (#e63946) used sparingly and with intent
4. No gradients, no glow effects — clean, editorial, confident
5. Generous whitespace — breathing room signals quality
6. Motion with purpose — subtle entrance animations only

---

## Logo Direction

- Wordmark: "SQUABBLE-UP" in all caps, bold condensed typeface
- Optional icon: glove silhouette, fist, or abstract mark
- Versions: Dark bg (white text), Light bg (dark text), Monochrome
- SVG-first, no raster logos
